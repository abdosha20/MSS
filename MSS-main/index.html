Our Goals

Manchester Sudanese Supplementary School is pioneering in entrenching the basics of Arabic and Islamic studies and culture in the city of Manchester. We have 30 years of experience with hard working staff and care excellence, efficient staff empowering children to achieve their full potential including GCSE classes. The courses we are providing not only enhance the Arabic language, but also helps them to gain a competitive and academic edge. Our doors are widely open to everyone from any nationality and background. We have an open and transparent communication policy for your child's performance aiming for their best achievement. We have a mix of classrooms and extracurricular activities, convenient location with car parking to drop off and pick up your children. Together we can make our future's leaders.
