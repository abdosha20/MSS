Admission Requirements

  • Children aged 5 and above are eligible to apply.
  • Completed application form (available at the school office).
  • Copy of birth certificate or passport.
  • Previous school report (if applicable).

Key Dates

  • August 15: Application deadline for new students
  • September 1: Start of new academic year
  • September 10: Welcome meeting for new families

Admission FAQ

Who can apply to the school?

We welcome students from all backgrounds, aged 5 and above.

When will I know if my child has been accepted?

We aim to notify families within two weeks of receiving the application.

Is there a waiting list?

If classes are full, we will place your child on a waiting list and contact you as soon as a place becomes available.

Can I visit the school before applying?

Yes, please contact us to arrange a visit or attend our open day events.
