Student FAQ

How do I join a club or activity?

Speak to your class teacher or visit the school office to sign up for clubs and activities.

Where can I get extra help with my studies?

Ask your teacher about after-school support sessions or tutoring options available.

What should I do if I am absent?

Ask a classmate or your teacher for any missed work and make sure to catch up as soon as possible.

Can I suggest a new club or activity?

Yes! Speak to your teacher or the school office with your idea and they will consider it.

How do I contact the school for help?

You can use the contact information at the bottom of this page or speak to your teacher directly.
