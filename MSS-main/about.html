About Us

Unlock your child's potential at the Manchester Sudanese Supplementary School. For three decades, we have proudly offered a rich education in Arabic, Islamic studies, and culture to the Manchester community. Our dedicated educators, including those who prepare students for GCSEs, are committed to helping every child succeed.

Our curriculum is designed to provide students with a competitive academic advantage and a deeper appreciation of the Arabic language. We are an inclusive school, with our doors wide open to children from all walks of life. We believe in a strong partnership with parents, underscored by our open communication about your child's achievements.

To further enrich their learning, we offer a variety of extracurricular activities. Our convenient location and on-site parking make drop-off and pick-up easy for families. Join us in building a brighter future for our children.

Academic Excellence

Our commitment to academic excellence is at the core of our educational mission. We provide a rigorous and comprehensive academic foundation in Arabic and Islamic studies, meticulously designed to help students excel not only in their formal education but also in their personal growth. Our curriculum challenges students to think critically, fosters intellectual curiosity, and equips them with the skills necessary for success in future academic pursuits, including GCSE examinations. Beyond grades, we focus on nurturing well-rounded individuals who are confident in their identity and possess a strong moral compass.

Inclusive Community

We believe our greatest strength lies in our community, a partnership built on collaboration between our students, their families, and our dedicated staff. Our school warmly welcomes children and families from all walks of life, creating a vibrant tapestry of different cultures and backgrounds that reflects the rich diversity of Manchester itself. This dynamic mix is what enriches our learning environment, fostering a culture of mutual respect, deep understanding, and lasting friendship. We are committed to ensuring every student feels safe, valued, and has a true sense of belonging from the moment they walk through our doors.
