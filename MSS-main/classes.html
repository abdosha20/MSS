Our Classes & Curriculum

At the Manchester Sudanese Supplementary School, our classes are the heart of our community. We offer a structured and engaging curriculum designed to guide students of all ages on a journey of linguistic and spiritual discovery. Our programmes are tailored to build a strong foundation in Arabic and Islamic Studies, nurturing confident, knowledgeable, and well-rounded individuals.

All classes are held on weekends during the school term, making it convenient for students to supplement their mainstream education.

Arabic Language Studies

Our Arabic programme builds confidence and fluency in reading, writing, speaking, and listening for all levels.

  • Beginner (Foundation): Master the Arabic alphabet, pronunciation, and basic vocabulary using engaging methods like Qaida Noorania.
  • Intermediate: Develop reading comprehension, expand vocabulary, and learn essential grammar to form sentences.
  • Advanced: Focus on fluency, advanced grammar (Nahw), morphology (Sarf), and conversational skills for engaging with Arabic texts and media.

Islamic Studies

A deep and balanced understanding of faith, values, and character in a modern British context.

  • Qur'anic Studies: Tajweed, memorisation (Hifz), and understanding (Tafsir).
  • Prophetic Teachings: Seerah and Hadith, drawing lessons for daily life.
  • Beliefs & Practices: Aqeedah & Fiqh—core beliefs and practical worship.
  • Character & Ethics: Akhlaq—developing honesty, compassion, and community spirit.

Specialist Classes

Tailored programmes for different age groups and academic goals.

  • GCSE Arabic: Dedicated Key Stage 4 programme, following national curriculum, preparing for formal qualification in all four skill areas.
  • Early Years Foundation (Ages 4-6): Play-based, interactive introduction to Arabic sounds, Islamic stories, and good manners through fun activities.

How to Enrol

We welcome new students throughout the year. To ensure your child is placed in the most suitable class for their age and ability, we conduct a simple and friendly assessment upon registration.

Please contact us to arrange a visit or to learn more about our enrolment process.
